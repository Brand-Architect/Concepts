The Opportunity

A brand with uncompromising standards — and an identity that can amplify it.

Origin Coffee is more than a roastery; it's a technical standard-bearer for the industry. Their commitment to full traceability and direct trade deserves a visual language that is as precise, clean, and intentional as the coffee they produce.

01

Technical Dilution

The technical precision of Origin's roasting is often lost in lifestyle imagery. The brand needs to reclaim its 'Expert' status through a more architectural visual language.

02

Traceability Gap

While Origin excels at direct trade, the depth of these relationships is often buried. The identity must make traceability the hero, not a footnote.
