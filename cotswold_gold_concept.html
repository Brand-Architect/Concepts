Digital Audit: Faults Found

Visual Hierarchy

The current site is product-heavy but story-light. It lacks a strong hero narrative that sells the brand's premium heritage before the transaction.

Cluttered Navigation

The menu structure is overwhelming. A premium brand should guide the user with curated paths, not a wall of options.

Mobile Friction

The mobile experience feels like a standard template. It lacks the 'touch of luxury' that high-end consumers expect when browsing on the go.

Performance Issues

Slow loading times and unoptimized assets (like the current logo) create friction that leads to high bounce rates among premium buyers.

Inconsistent Typography

The mix of fonts lacks a cohesive 'voice'. We propose a pairing of Playfair Display and Montserrat for a timeless, authoritative feel.

The Solution

A headless commerce approach with a focus on cinematic storytelling, lightning-fast performance, and a 'less is more' design philosophy.
