The Founder's Story

From Exeter hills to
your breakfast table.

Butter Bike Co. isn't just about peanut butter; it's about the journey. Jeni started roasting nuts in her kitchen, experimenting with flavors that were bold, natural, and exciting.

Today, every jar is still made with that same artisanal spirit. We use high-oleic peanuts, a touch of sea salt, and a whole lot of passion. No palm oil, no nonsense.
