Critical

The 'Lake District' Trap

The current logo uses standard mountain imagery that is ubiquitous in the region. It lacks a unique identity and fails to stand out in a crowded specialty market.

Critical

Fragmented Naming

Mixing 'Penningtons', 'Penningtons Coffee Roasters', and 'Tea & Coffee LTD' creates confusion. A single, strong name is required for national growth.

Critical

Cluttered Experience

The current website entry is a wall of text and functional notices. It undersells the premium, artisanal quality of your award-winning roast.
