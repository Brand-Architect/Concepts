Independent Website Assessment · April 2026

What Your Current Site
Isn't Doing For You

A frank review of forgecoffeeroasters.co.uk — and what a new site could achieve.

FORGE Coffee Roasters

Their current logo — type only, no mark, no identity anchor

Visual Identity

Logo & Branding

  • Text-only logotype with no distinctive mark — instantly forgettable
  • No visual anchor or symbol a customer can recognise at a glance
  • Anyone could replicate this with a font — it owns nothing
  • No consistent colour palette applied across the site
  • Brand personality (industrial craft, Sheffield steel) not reflected
Hero & First Impression

Above The Fold

  • Hero image is cinematic but the logo gets lost in the dark photo
  • No immediate call-to-action — visitors don't know what to do next
  • Quote is compelling but set too small and too light to land
  • The old van (a brilliant brand story) is not celebrated or visible
  • Sheffield, steel, fire and craft are not felt at first glance
Navigation & UX

Structure & Flow

  • "THE FORGE" nav item links back to the homepage — confusing label
  • Cart uses an emoji 🛒 — cheap against a premium brand positioning
  • No sticky navigation — the logo vanishes entirely as you scroll
  • Duplicate nav elements appear twice in the page source
  • No search function visible from the store
Copy & Content

Story & Messaging

  • Typo on the homepage: "ur coffee is hand crafted" — missing the O
  • The Sheffield heritage story is powerful but buried in walls of text
  • Seven values listed — too many, none have visual weight or hierarchy
  • No founder story — Simon's passion for the craft is invisible
  • No testimonials, press coverage or third-party trust signals
E-Commerce

Selling Coffee Online

  • No coffee products visible on the homepage — no path to purchase
  • Subscription offer — highest lifetime value — is not featured at all
  • No seasonal, limited or new roast messaging anywhere
  • Tasting notes, roast dates and origin are buried deep in the store
  • No urgency or social proof near the add-to-basket button
Technical & SEO

Speed & Findability

  • Page title "The Forge - Forge Coffee" is repetitive and weak for SEO
  • Hero image not optimised — slow load on mobile connections
  • No structured data (schema.org) for products or local business
  • Squarespace limits meaningful performance optimisation
  • No mobile-first layout consideration below 480px
